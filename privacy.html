Privacy Policy

This application helps small service businesses manage appointment bookings through WhatsApp.

Data We Collect

We may process the customer's WhatsApp phone number, name, selected service, selected day and selected time.

How We Use the Data

The data is used only to process and manage appointment bookings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed through Meta / WhatsApp Business Platform as part of the messaging service.

Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their appointment data by contacting us.

We handle deletion requests promptly and confirm once the data is removed.
